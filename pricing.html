Pricing FAQs

It's always hard to choose new company tools. We're here to help you make the right decision.

How much does a subscription cost?

Each seat costs $1000/month. Each seat contains 200 exclusive high-propensity leads around your zip code of choice.

How often will I receive leads?

You will receive 200 leads upon signing up, as well as an additional 200 on the 1st of every month.

Are other Advisors receiving the same leads that I am?

No, every lead that you receive is exclusive to you, forever.

When will I get charged?

You will get charged on the anniversary of your signup date.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes, dependent on the location and quantity, we do offer discounts. Reach out to us to discuss!

Can I cancel at anytime?

Yes, unless you selected the 12-month payment method, you can cancel at any time.
